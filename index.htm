About me

Graphic/Web Designer

I am a Web Designer and Graphic artist based in east Kent. I enjoy creating smart, entertaining and functional web sites and eye catching branding.

Responsive

Responsive design is a huge part of modern website design. Recent statistics reveal that mobile’s share of global web traffic leapt 39% since the same time last year, with one-third of all web pages now served to mobile phones. In my opinion not building mobile first websites is kinda silly.

Mobile first is a fun challenge, thinking outside the box and having to create two if not more designs of the same page depending on the display.

Creative

I love being creative. I’m always excited in producing things that grab attention, are a bit different and generally entertaining.

I’m a bit of a perfectionist and hate having to put up with ‘good enough’, which generally means I’m never happy with what I’ve done, but does mean I can produce some nice things.

Functional

A website or a graphic design is useless if it does not fit its intended purpose, a bit of a no-brainer but any design which looks amazing and shiny but makes you think, ‘wait… What is this about?’ is a bad design, I don’t like that.

Also things like screens which show twitter feeds on fridges. Why? It is essentially useless. No one is going to buy a £2000 fridge to look at their twitter feed, because they’ve probably already got an expensive ipad for that at this point. However, fuck yeah internet fridge feed is amazing.
